What is COVID policy at the venue?

Proof of full vaccination is now required by the City of San Francisco to enter the venue. The following are acceptable as proof of full vaccination, along with a photo ID: (i) the actual CDC vaccination card, which includes name of person vaccinated, type of vaccine provided, and date last dose administered, or similar documentation issued by another foreign governmental jurisdiction, (ii) a photo of the vaccination card as a separate document, or a photo of the vaccination card stored on a phone or electronic device, (iii) a QR Digital Code of your COVID-19 Vaccine Record
